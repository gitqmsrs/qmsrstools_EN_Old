Customer Order Processing Activities
1- Purpose
This process covers how an order is processed from receipt through delivery to customer.
Every effort will be made to ensure that services are in accordance with specification and reach the customer in a
satisfactory condition.
2- Scope of application
 This procedure spans the interval from the receipt of the client’s request .
 Only orders received by Fax, EDI, post, online or E-mail is accepted.
 All orders are checked prior to processing them.
 All orders are entered on a computer system following proper authorisation.
 Orders may only be amended or cancelled following proper authorisation.
3- Responsibilities
 It is the responsibility of each person in the company to ensure that the customer request is met.
 Invoicing Team.
 Order Processing Team.
