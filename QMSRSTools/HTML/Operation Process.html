Operation Process
1- Purpose
This process defines the Operation activities and the processes involved in managing the
operations inside the company.
2- Scope of application
 This procedure covers the main activities of the operation processes inside QMSrs.
 The operation processes include: HR, Accounting, Purchasing and the
communication in both directions: externally and internally.
