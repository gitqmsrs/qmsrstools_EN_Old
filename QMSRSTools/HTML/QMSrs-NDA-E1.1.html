QMS Resourcing Services Limited (There after QMSrs) and the company wish to discuss supply, support,
development and marketing collaboration ("the Project") and recognise that each of them is or will be in
possession of confidential information relating to the Project, and the businesses and business secrets of
each of them ("the Confidential Information"). QMSrs and the company wish to ensure that no
unauthorized use or disclosure is made of the Confidential Information.
The Confidential Information shall include but not be limited to:
(a) any business secrets or confidential information relating or belonging to either party including,
but not limited to, information about sales or marketing plans, business plans, financial
information, suppliers and delivery schedules;
(b) designs, drawings, technical specifications, performance data, manufacturing techniques, know-
how, patent or other registerable right applications or specifications relating thereto or other
intellectual property rights;
(c) any source code or object code or other software of any nature whatsoever;
(d) any information obtained through inspection of products, components or apparatus developed or
manufactured by or on behalf of either party or from instructions for use, servicing or repair of
products or components;
and all and each and every part of such information.
1. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as compelling either party to disclose any
information to the other.
2. Confidential Information belonging to QMSrs or the company as appropriate ("the Disclosing
Party") and received by the company or QMSrs as appropriate ("the Receiving Party") may only
be used or disclosed by the Receiving Party for the purposes of the Projects and in accordance
with the terms of this Agreement.
3. The Receiving Party agrees:
(a) not to use the Confidential Information or any part thereof for their own purposes except for
the purposes of the Projects;
(b) to take all reasonable precautions and care to ensure that such information is kept entirely
secret and confidential and not to divulge at any time the Confidential Information or any part
thereof to any third party unless and until the Disclosing Party has given its express written
consent to do so and the third party has entered into a confidentially undertaking direct with
the Disclosing Party;
(c) to disclose the Confidential Information only to those of its employees and service contractors
to whom such disclosure is necessary for the conduct of the Projects and to ensure before
disclosing to them any of the Confidential Information that each such employee or service
contractor is made aware of the obligations as to confidentiality which are binding upon the
Receiving Party and such employees and service contractors;
(d) to notify the Disclosing Party of all parties and persons who have access to the Confidential
Information;
(e) not at any time to copy, whether in whole or in part, or to adapt in any way the Confidential
Information for any purpose without the prior written consent of the Disclosing Party.

4. The obligations of confidentiality in this Agreement shall not apply to any information which:
(a) is already, or becomes, in the public domain at, or after, the date of disclosure by either party
other than as a result of breach by the Receiving Party of its obligations hereunder;
(b) is obtained by the Receiving Party from a third party who is legitimately in possession of such
information, has a right to disclose it and is not under any obligation of confidentiality to
either party;
(c) shall be required to be disclosed to any competent legal or other regulatory authority. In this
instance the Disclosing Party must be notified by the Receiving Party prior to disclosure to
any regulatory body.
5. This Agreement will terminate three (3) years from the date hereof or upon 30 days written notice
from either party. Upon termination all confidentiality restrictions herein will continue to apply for
a further 3 years. The Disclosing Party may require the return of the Confidential Information
provided to the Receiving Party, and all copies thereof in which case the Receiving Party shall
forthwith either return or destroy all Confidential Information of any kind held by or on behalf of
the Receiving Party whether or not in the possession of the Receiving Party, and will confirm in
writing that all such Confidential Information and related information has been returned or
destroyed.
6. The Disclosing Party acknowledges that disclosure of the Confidential Information gives it no
rights in respect of any product, process or intellectual property which the Receiving Party may
develop solely as a result of the Receiving Party's own research and development conducted
without reference to the Confidential Information.
7. The obligations of the Receiving Party under this Agreement are in addition to any other
obligations which the Receiving Party may have under statute, common law or otherwise. The
provisions of this Agreement are without prejudice to any other right or remedy which the
Disclosing Party may have in connection with the use or disclosure of the Confidential
Information.
8. The parties acknowledge that damages would not be an adequate remedy for a breach of the terms
of this Agreement and further agree that the Disclosing Party shall (in such circumstances) be
entitled to equitable relief including injunctions.
9. Both parties agree that this Agreement does not grant any license, implied or otherwise, or create
any other rights or obligations than those herein.
10. This Agreement constitutes the complete agreement between the parties in relation to the
Confidential Information and shall not be varied without the prior written consent of both parties.
11. The construction and performance of this Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England
and the parties accept the exclusive jurisdiction of the English courts (Both Parties will try to settle
any issues amicably within a reasonable time period of (120 Days) before bringing to court)
